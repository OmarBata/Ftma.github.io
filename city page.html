About NEORISE



NEORISE is a cutting_edge smart city project located in Cairo ,envisioned and initiated in 2022,designed as a model for future urban development ,NEORISE integrates advanced digital infrastructure, smart transportation, the city features, high_speed connectivity, green public spaces, and Al-powered services that cater to residents' needs efficiently. With a focus on innovation, and community well-being, NEORISE aims to set new standars for how cities of the future are built and experienced.
